# 15 - Competitive Learnings: AIZdravo.com

## What is AIZdravo?

AI learning platform for Balkan professionals. Subscription-based (€15/month).
Relevant because: similar region, similar language (BHS), similar business model
(subscription tiers + community + content library).

Source: Website analysis + screenshots of authenticated dashboard, profile,
and community sections (March 2026).

### 2. Personalized Journey ("Moja Personalizovana putanja")

AIZdravo creates a personalized learning path based on quiz results.

**For Šarena Sfera: "Razvojna putanja djeteta"**
- Based on child's age, assessments, and observations
- Shows: current developmental stage per domain
- Recommends: next workshops, home activities, resources
- Timeline view: past milestones achieved → current focus → upcoming goals
- Adapts as staff enters new observations
- Different path per child

### 3. Progress Tracking ("Moj napredak")

AIZdravo shows user's learning progress.

**For Šarena Sfera: "Moj napredak" (for parents)**
- Workshops attended (this month / total)
- Home activities completed
- Engagement score (how active is the parent)
- Child's development progress summary
- Badges earned
- Streak: "3 sedmice zaredom na radionicama!"

### 4. Referral System ("Preporuči prijatelju")

AIZdravo has a dedicated referral page.

**For Šarena Sfera: "Preporuči prijatelja"**
- Unique referral link per parent
- Reward: 1 month free Paid tier for each successful referral
- Referred parent gets: 1 month free Paid trial
- Tracking: how many referred, how many converted
- Shareable on WhatsApp, Viber, Facebook, Instagram
- "Roditelji Pioniri" badge for 3+ referrals

### 5. Certificates ("Sertifikati")

AIZdravo awards certificates for completed courses.

**For Šarena Sfera: Certificates & Achievements**

**For children:**
- "Dječiji pasoš" certificate at end of 12-month program
- Domain mastery certificates (e.g. "Kreativni istraživač")
- Attendance milestones (25, 50, 75, 96 workshops)
- Printable + shareable PDF certificates

**For parents:**
- "Roditelj Pionir" — early adopter
- "Aktivni roditelj" — completed 10+ home activities
- "Zajednica" — contributed to forum 5+ times
- "Ambasador" — referred 3+ families

### 6. Community Forum (Zajednica)

AIZdravo has a forum with:
- Category tabs (AI opšti, Industrije, Pomoć, Tutoriali)
- Thread list with author, date, replies, views
- "Nova diskusija" button
- Trending topics sidebar
- Top contributors sidebar with avatars + post counts
- Sorting: Najnovije, Najgledanije

**For Šarena Sfera: "Zajednica roditelja" (Premium feature)**
```
Categories:
- Opšte diskusije          (general parenting)
- Po uzrastu (2-3, 3-4, 4-5, 5-6)
- Po domenu (6 domains)
- Kućne aktivnosti         (share ideas)
- Pitaj stručnjaka         (expert Q&A, Premium)
- Savjeti i iskustva       (tips & experiences)

Features:
- Trending topics sidebar
- Top contributors (active parents)
- Pin important posts (admin)
- Photo sharing in posts
- Expert-verified answers (badge on expert replies)
```

### 7. Content Library ("Biblioteka")

AIZdravo has 300+ tools organized as a searchable library.

**For Šarena Sfera: "Biblioteka aktivnosti"**
- 200+ activities organized by:
  - Domain (emotional, social, creative, cognitive, motor, language)
  - Age range (2-3, 3-4, 4-5, 5-6)
  - Type (indoor, outdoor, creative, physical, sensory)
  - Duration (5min, 15min, 30min)
  - Materials needed (none, basic, specific)
- Each activity card shows:
  - Title, domain color, age range
  - Short description
  - Materials needed
  - Step-by-step instructions
  - Photo/illustration
  - Difficulty level
  - "Uradi i podijeli" (Do it and share) button
- Free tier: 10 activities
- Paid: 50 activities
- Premium: All activities

### 8. Dashboard Card Layout

AIZdravo uses video thumbnail cards in a grid for content discovery.

**For Šarena Sfera dashboard:**
- Cards for each child (with mini radar chart)
- Cards for upcoming workshops (with cover image)
- Cards for recommended activities
- Cards for recent observations
- "Šta je novo" section (new content, features)

### 9. Profile with Role + Subscription Visible

AIZdravo shows: Rola (USER), Pretplata (PREMIUM) clearly on profile.

**For Šarena Sfera:**
- Show tier badge in profile header: "Free", "Osnovni", "Premium"
- Show role: "Roditelj", "Voditelj", "Admin"
- Quick upgrade link if not Premium

   [Radoznalo / Stidljivo / Plačljivo / Zavisi]

### 11. "Roditelji Pioniri" Program (inspired by AIZdravo "Founding Members")

AIZdravo offers "Founding Member" status with lifetime locked pricing.

**For Šarena Sfera: "Roditelji Pioniri"**
- First 50 families to join
- Benefits:
  - Lifetime price lock: 10 KM/mj instead of 15 KM (Paid) or 20 KM instead of 30 KM (Premium)
  - "Roditelj Pionir" badge on profile (permanent)
  - Priority access to new features
  - Direct feedback channel with team
  - Name on "Zid pionira" (Pioneer Wall) on website
- Creates urgency: "Samo 12 mjesta preostalo!"
- Counter on landing page showing spots remaining

### 12. Partner/Affiliate Program

AIZdravo has "Partnerski program".

**For Šarena Sfera (Phase 2+):**
- Paediatricians, kindergartens, schools can become partners
- Partner gets unique code
- Partner earns: 20% of first month subscription for each referral
- Or: free group license for referring 10+ families
- Partner badge on platform
- Co-branded certificates
